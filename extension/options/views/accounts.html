Connect with the following services

  • Facebook

    Nimble gives you the option to share any webpage you are on to Facebook, or any text that you have highlighted on the screen. It is extremely easy to use even if there are no built-in share to Facebook buttons. No more hassle of switching to Facebook

  • Dropbox

    Dropbox is a free service that lets you bring your photos, docs, and videos anywhere and share them easily.There are many things we see on webpages that we would like to archive and refer later. Dropbox is the perfect service to save images and media files.

  • Twilio

    Twilio provides communication at your fingertips! Select some text or a link and send it to a US mobile number. It is as easy as that!

  • Pastebin

    Pastebin.com is a website where you can store text for a certain period of time. The website is mainly used by programmers to store pieces of sources code or configuration information, but anyone is more than welcome to paste any type of text. The idea behind the site is to make it more convenient for people to share large amounts of text online. Share any content to Pastebin instantly and conveniently using Nimble!
